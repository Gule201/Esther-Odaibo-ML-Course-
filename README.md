## Esther Odaibo – AI in Healthcare Portfolio

**Course:** ITAI-4375: AI in Healthcare  
**Instructor:** Dr. Doreen Rosenstrauch  

### About Me
I am a Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner (PMHNP) and Registered Nurse with a strong interest in integrating artificial intelligence into healthcare to improve patient outcomes, enhance clinical decision-making, and support data-driven care models.

This portfolio showcases my work throughout this course, demonstrating my understanding of AI applications in healthcare, including predictive analytics, clinical decision support systems, natural language processing, and reinforcement learning.
