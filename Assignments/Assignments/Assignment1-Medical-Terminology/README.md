# Assignment 1: Medical Terminology in Healthcare

## Problem
This assignment focused on identifying important medical and healthcare terms commonly used in modern clinical and AI-related healthcare literature.

## Approach
I selected key healthcare terminology, defined each term, explained its context, and discussed why it is important in healthcare practice and AI integration.

## Results
This assignment highlighted how terms such as EHR, CDS, machine learning, interoperability, algorithmic bias, and NLP are foundational to understanding AI in healthcare.

## What I Learned
I learned that precise terminology is essential for effective communication in healthcare and for understanding how AI technologies are applied in clinical settings.
