# Assignment 3: Clinical Decision Support Systems (CDSS)

## Problem
This assignment explored the future role of AI-powered clinical decision support systems in healthcare.

## Approach
I described a future scenario in which CDSS tools are integrated into clinical workflows and compared that vision to current systems.

## Results
The assignment showed how AI-powered CDSS could improve diagnostic accuracy, reduce clinician burden, and support more personalized care.

## What I Learned
I learned that the future of CDSS depends on balancing intelligent automation with transparency, ethics, and clinician oversight.
