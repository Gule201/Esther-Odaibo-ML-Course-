# Assignment 6: Exploring Non-Traditional Health Data

## Problem
This assignment explored genetic testing as a form of non-traditional health data in healthcare.

## Approach
I examined how genetic data supports precision medicine, population health, and AI-driven analytics, while also considering privacy and ethical concerns.

## Results
The assignment showed that genetic testing can improve risk identification and clinical decision-making when supported by strong governance and counseling.

## What I Learned
I learned that non-traditional health data can be valuable in healthcare, but its use must be balanced with privacy, ethics, and patient protection.
