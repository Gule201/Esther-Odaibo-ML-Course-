# Assignment 4: Understanding a Medical Diagnostic Test

## Problem
This assignment examined the purpose, reliability, sensitivity, specificity, and limitations of a rapid COVID-19 antigen test.

## Approach
I reviewed how the test works, when it is most useful, and the implications of false positives and false negatives.

## Results
The assignment showed that rapid antigen tests are useful for quick detection, especially in symptomatic individuals, though they are less sensitive than PCR tests.

## What I Learned
I learned how diagnostic tests are evaluated and why sensitivity and specificity are important in clinical decision-making.
