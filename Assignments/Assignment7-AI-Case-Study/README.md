# Assignment 7: AI in a Medical Case Study

## Problem
This assignment involved identifying major health risks in a sepsis case study and explaining how AI could reduce those risks.

## Approach
I analyzed the clinical case, identified key risks, and described how predictive analytics and clinical decision support could improve early intervention.

## Results
The assignment demonstrated that AI can support earlier detection of sepsis, reduce delays in treatment, and potentially improve patient outcomes.

## What I Learned
I learned how AI tools can be applied in urgent clinical scenarios to strengthen risk recognition and support timely care.
